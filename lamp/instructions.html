Basic Instructions for First Time Users:


RETURN TO HOME


How to Login.

To login, enter your invitation email and your passkey. Aftering logging in, you should immediately be able to browse profiles.

Browsing Profiles.

You can browse through profiles by clicking or tapping on the right and left arrows. To find out more about a person, click or tap on the "Why Me" button.


Managing Your Profile.

To manage your profile, click or tap on the Settings link at the top of the screen. On mobile phones or smaller screens, this link may be collapsed under a menu icon.


Submitting Offers.

As you browse through profiles, you can submit a Work, Friend, or Love offer. Just click or tap on the Work, Friend or Love button and then enter the amount of EOS you want to offer.


Viewing Matches.

If you have matches, you can use the New link at the top of the screen. On mobile phones or smaller screens, this link may be collapsed under a menu icon.

Accepting Matches.

On the View Match page, you have the option to accept or reject a match. If you accept, you will move to the smart contract.

Accepting Contracts.

If a contract has not been created, you first will select its type. It is recommended to accept the default if it's your first contract. Once a contract is selected, you send testnet EOS from your alpha account to the contract by clicking the "Send EOS" button.

Managing Contracts.

Once you are in a contract, you enter the interaction on the View Step page. Enter your text and then click the "Submit Step" button at the bottom of the page. To terminate or arbitrate a contract, use the corresponding buttons.
